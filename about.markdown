My entry into IT came from diving headfirst into real-world environments, working on and troubleshooting issues in large-scale Windows systems. Since then, I’ve continued developing my skills both professionally and independently by building home lab environments that mirror real-world infrastructure.

## Technical Experience

My professional experience includes:

- Windows environments and system troubleshooting
- Linux server deployments and administration

I’ve built complete environments from the ground up, including:

- Reverse proxies (Nginx)

I’m especially interested in how **systems, networking, and security intersect**, and how small misconfigurations can lead to larger issues.
